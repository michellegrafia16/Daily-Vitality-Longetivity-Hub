Nutrition & Diet Guidance

Evidence-based dietary strategies to fuel your body for longevity and vitality.

🥑

The Longevity Diet

Research from the Blue Zones reveals that populations living longest share common dietary patterns. Focus on whole plant foods, moderate protein, and healthy fats.

🥗

Mediterranean Approach

Rich in olive oil, fish, vegetables, and whole grains. This dietary pattern is associated with reduced cardiovascular disease and cognitive decline.

⏰

Time-Restricted Eating

Consuming food within an 8-10 hour window can improve metabolic health, reduce inflammation, and support cellular repair processes.

🧬

Personalized Nutrition

Genetic testing and biomarker analysis can help tailor your diet to your unique needs for optimal health outcomes.

Key Nutritional Principles

Protein

Aim for 1.0-1.2g per kg of body weight daily. Include diverse sources: fish, legumes, dairy, and plant-based proteins.

Fiber

Target 25-35g daily from vegetables, fruits, whole grains, and legumes for gut health and stable blood sugar.

Healthy Fats

Prioritize omega-3s from fatty fish, nuts, seeds, and olive oil. Limit processed oils and trans fats.
