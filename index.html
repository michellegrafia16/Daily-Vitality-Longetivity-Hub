The Science

The Science of Aging

Explore the latest research in longevity science and discover how you can optimize your healthspan through evidence-based interventions.

Cellular Senescence

Senescent cells accumulate as we age, contributing to chronic inflammation. Strategies like senolytics and lifestyle interventions can help clear these cells and improve tissue function.

Telomere Biology

Telomeres protect our DNA and shorten with each cell division. Research shows that stress reduction, proper nutrition, and regular exercise can help maintain telomere length.

Mitochondrial Health

Mitochondria are the powerhouses of our cells. Supporting them through interval training, adequate sleep, and specific nutrients can enhance energy production and reduce aging markers.
