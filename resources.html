Recommended Reading

📚 "Blue Zones" by Dan Buettner

Explores the world's longest-lived populations and their common lifestyle habits. Practical insights from the Blue Zones regions.

📚 "How Not to Die" by Dr. Michael Greger

Evidence-based nutrition guide focusing on foods that prevent and reverse disease. Scientific research made accessible.

📚 "The Power of Now" by Eckhart Tolle

A guide to spiritual enlightenment and present moment awareness. Transformative concepts for mental clarity.

📚 "Atomic Habits" by James Clear

Practical strategies for building good habits and breaking bad ones. Small changes that lead to remarkable results.

📚 "Why We Sleep" by Matthew Walker

The science of sleep and its profound impact on health, cognitive function, and longevity. Essential reading for everyone.

📚 "The Span" by Dr. Peter Attia

Outlines the strategies for extending lifespan and improving healthspan through exercise, nutrition, and sleep optimization.
