Complement Your Supplements with These Habits

Nutrition Tips

  • Prioritize protein with every meal
  • Eat plenty of fiber-rich vegetables
  • Stay hydrated with water throughout the day
  • Reduce processed foods and added sugars
  • Practice mindful eating

Exercise Recommendations

  • Incorporate strength training 3-4x per week
  • Add 30 minutes of cardio daily
  • Include high-intensity interval training (HIIT)
  • Take daily walks
  • Stretch to prevent injury

Lifestyle Factors

  • Get 7-9 hours of quality sleep
  • Manage stress through meditation or yoga
  • Track your progress
  • Be consistent - results take time
