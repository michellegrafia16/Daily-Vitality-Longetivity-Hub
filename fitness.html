Fitness & Movement

Exercise strategies to keep your body strong, agile, and resilient at every age.

Movement for Longevity

🚶

Daily Walking

10,000 steps daily is associated with reduced all-cause mortality. Make walking a non-negotiable part of your routine.

🏋️

Strength Training

Preserve muscle mass with 2-3 strength sessions weekly. Resistance training fights sarcopenia and supports bone health.

🧘

Flexibility & Balance

Yoga and balance exercises reduce fall risk and improve mobility. Essential for maintaining independence as you age.

💨

High-Intensity Intervals

Brief bursts of intense exercise boost mitochondria and improve cardiovascular efficiency. Just 10-20 minutes counts.

Weekly Exercise Guide

🗓️ Monday - Upper Body

  • Push-ups: 3 sets x 10 reps
  • Dumbbell rows: 3 x 12
  • Shoulder press: 3 x 10
  • Plank: 3 x 30 sec

🗓️ Tuesday - Cardio

  • 30 min brisk walking or cycling
  • 5 min warm-up and cool-down
  • Option: Add 10 min HIIT

🗓️ Wednesday - Lower Body

  • Squats: 3 x 12
  • Lunges: 3 x 10 each leg
  • Calf raises: 3 x 15
  • Deadlifts: 3 x 10

🗓️ Thursday - Rest & Recovery

  • Light stretching: 20 min
  • Yoga or mobility work
  • Optional: gentle walk

🗓️ Friday - Full Body

  • Burpees: 3 x 8
  • Jump squats: 3 x 10
  • Mountain climbers: 3 x 30 sec
  • Resistance training circuit

🗓️ Weekend - Active Recovery

  • 60 min outdoor activity
  • Hiking, swimming, or cycling
  • Family movement activities
